# Project Brief

## Project Name
Coach Connect

## Overview
Coach Connect is a comprehensive platform designed for football/soccer coaches, clubs, scouts, and trainers. It provides a suite of tools for training planning, tactical analysis, professional networking, and career development.

## Core Value Proposition
A centralized platform that empowers football coaches with professional tools for training management, tactical planning, and career advancement while connecting them with the broader football community.

## Target Users
- **Coaches**: Individual coaches at various levels (UEFA licensed)
- **Clubs**: Football clubs managing multiple staff members
- **Scouts**: Talent identification professionals
- **Trainers**: Fitness and specialized trainers
- **Admins**: Platform administrators

## Key Features

### 1. Exercise Library
- Create, share, and discover training exercises
- Categorized by type (passing, shooting, tactical, etc.)
- Age group appropriate (U7 through senior)
- Difficulty levels and equipment requirements
- Premium content support

### 2. Training Planner
- Season planning (2-week, monthly, 3-month, full season)
- Training session scheduling
- Exercise organization within sessions
- Club-wide plan management

### 3. Tactic Board
- Visual tactical planning tool
- Animation capabilities
- Save and share tactical diagrams

### 4. Social & Networking
- Professional feed with posts and insights
- User connections and following
- Match reports and tactical insights sharing

### 5. Messaging System
- Direct messaging between users
- Conversation management
- Media sharing

### 6. Job Board
- Job listings from clubs
- Application management
- Various coaching positions (head coach, assistant, GK coach, etc.)

### 7. Forum
- Category-based discussions
- Community knowledge sharing
- Pinned and locked threads

### 8. Match Management
- Match scheduling and tracking
- Friendly match finder/matchmaker
- Match requests and acceptance

### 9. Marketplace
- Service listings (private training, video analysis, consulting)
- Equipment sales
- Featured listings

### 10. Club Management
- Club profiles
- Staff management
- Multi-account support

## Subscription Tiers
- **Free**: Basic access
- **Premium Coach**: Enhanced features for individual coaches
- **Pro Service**: Professional service providers
- **Club License**: Club-wide access with multiple accounts

## Success Metrics
- User engagement with exercise library
- Training plan creation and usage
- Professional connections made
- Job placements through platform
- Subscription conversions
